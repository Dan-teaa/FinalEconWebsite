Our Mission

To empower students by advancing economic understanding, fostering analytical debate, and providing direct access to industry leaders. Through skill-building initiatives, impactful publications, and strategic partnerships, we bridge the academic and professional worlds to shape future economists and informed leaders.

About Us

Welcome to the Economics Society at Fordham University's Lincoln Center, a dynamic, student-led organization dedicated to advancing economic literacy, fostering meaningful dialogue, and bridging the gap between academia and industry. Our mission is to empower students to think critically about the economic forces shaping our world and equip them with the tools to become informed leaders and changemakers.

We are a hub for students passionate about understanding complex economic issues. Through a combination of engaging events, insightful publications, and hands-on learning opportunities, the Econ Society serves as a platform for intellectual growth and professional development. From tackling global challenges to exploring emerging economic trends, we aim to turn classroom theories into real-world expertise.

What We Offer


  • Professional Development: Participate in networking sessions and mentorship programs designed to prepare you for a successful career in economics and beyond.
  • Publications That Inform: Explore our thought-provoking publications, including research reports, policy briefs, and op-eds that offer fresh perspectives on pressing economic issues.
  • Collaborative Community: Join a supportive network of peers who share your passion for learning and problem-solving, fostering collaboration and innovation at every step.
